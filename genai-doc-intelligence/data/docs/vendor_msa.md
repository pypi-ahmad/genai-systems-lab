# Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement is entered into on February 15, 2026 between Meridian Retail Group and Orion Data Systems.

Term and renewal:
- Initial term is twenty-four months beginning March 1, 2026.
- The agreement renews automatically for successive twelve-month periods unless either party gives sixty days written notice before the renewal date.

Fees and payment:
- Orion Data Systems will provide analytics infrastructure support for a monthly fee of $18,500.
- Invoices are due net 30 from the invoice date.
- Undisputed late balances accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

Service levels:
- Critical incidents must receive an initial response within one hour and a workaround within eight hours.
- Monthly uptime target is 99.9%, excluding scheduled maintenance announced at least seventy-two hours in advance.

Termination:
- Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach remains uncured for thirty days after written notice.
- Meridian Retail Group may terminate for convenience after the first twelve months with ninety days written notice.

Security and compliance:
- Orion Data Systems must maintain SOC 2 Type II certification throughout the agreement term.
- Customer data may not be stored outside the United States without prior written consent.
