Vendor evaluation notes for knowledge tooling shortlist.

Northstar Search offers fast semantic retrieval and built-in reranking, but pricing rises sharply once document volume exceeds ten million tokens.

Atlas Graph Studio is weaker on search UX but strong at entity linking, relationship traversal, and permission-aware metadata. It may fit compliance-heavy use cases better than general research workflows.

In-house vector search remains cheapest and easiest to customize. The tradeoff is that we need our own observability, indexing jobs, freshness checks, and evaluation harness.

Decision criteria for the next review:
- citation quality under ambiguous questions
- ingestion reliability for markdown, text, and policy documents
- access control by team and project
- cost per thousand queries under mixed workloads
